Privacy Policy Content

Introduction

Welcome to Reakgona Tours! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, booking details, and travel preferences. This data is collected only when you voluntarily provide it during booking inquiries, newsletter signups, or customer support interactions.

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us process bookings, send confirmations and updates, personalize your travel experience, and improve our services. We never sell, rent, or share your data with unauthorized third parties. Data is used solely for service delivery and communication.

Data Security

All sensitive information is encrypted during transmission and stored securely on protected servers. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only. We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains safe from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights & Contact Us

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Questions or concerns? Reach out at enquiries@reakgonatours.co.za or call 079 098 8801. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
